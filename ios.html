Butterfly Network - iOS

I worked as an iOS engineer at Butterfly Network for over a year. Butterfly Network makes handheld portable ultrasound devices that plug into iPhones and iPads. This makes the iOS app an integral part of the business. I worked with product and clinical partners to bring the veterinarian and enterprise offering to the market, amongst other things. I thoroughly enjoyed my time working there as an iOS engineer. Scroll down to get a glimpse of the app. To read more about my experience check out my resume.
The Butterfly iQ is a handheld ultrasound device which you interface with through the iQ app.
The iQ app has the ability to scan the entire body through an intuitive and easy to use interface.
After the images are captured, they can be saved and uploaded to the cloud to selected archives.
The images captured can be annotated and reviewed by clinicians right on the app.
